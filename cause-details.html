Feature Highlight

Real-Time Volunteer Assistance

Empowering accessibility

Join the Beta

SolSight’s real-time assistance feature connects visually impaired users with volunteers through live video sessions. Our platform focuses on trust, empathy, and reliability — ensuring users receive timely support for everyday challenges, powered by Web3 transparency.

Using a decentralized reputation system, volunteers build credibility through positive feedback and verified sessions, creating a sustainable ecosystem of accessibility and impact.

Our vision is to blend human compassion with decentralized systems — where technology doesn’t replace empathy, but amplifies it. SolSight will soon introduce advanced accessibility verification, real-time trust scoring, and AI-powered assistance to make every interaction seamless.
  • Connect users and volunteers instantly via live video
  • Enable decentralized reputation tracking using Web3
  • Reward genuine impact with reputation tiers
  • Ensure data privacy and transparency through blockchain
  • Promote inclusive participation for visually impaired users

About SolSight

SolSight is a Web3 accessibility platform connecting visually impaired users with volunteers through live, real-time guidance and decentralized reputation rewards.
