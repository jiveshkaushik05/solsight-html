Welcome to SolSight.

We Help the Visually Impaired See Through Technology.

We connect hundreds of visually impaired users with volunteers every day.

SolSight is a Web3-powered accessibility platform that connects visually impaired users with volunteers through live, real-time support. Every session strengthens human connection, builds empathy, and rewards impact using decentralized reputation.

  • Empowering vision through community and technology.
  • Building trust through transparency and Web3 innovation.
  • Turning compassion into measurable impact.
